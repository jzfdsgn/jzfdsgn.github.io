Egman’s OP11 Half Case Tourney

June 11, 2025 at 11:36 AM

Yesterday Egman hosted his half case tourney, one of our first big OP11 events. While it’s not an official event, it’s still a good peek into the current playing field of the meta. Especially considering the sim hadn’t been updated with the new cards prior to the event.

Here’s the link to Egmans’s report.

It’s not surprising that UP Luffy and Belo Betty make up a majority of the pie chart. What is surprising is the diversity in the rest of the leaders played. And even more surprising is that Lucci won the whole event. The finals were Lucci vs. Belo Betty.

It’s notable that Lucci did not run into any Buggy’s the entire tournament, which is supposed to be a really bad matchup for the cat.

It gives me some hope to see BP Luffy with a strong showing, coming in 3rd. Not to mention there are 2 of them in the Top 8.

Now I feel even more unsure of what to play for Vegas. So I asked my buddy Claude (AI) to give me the conversion rates of each leader in the top 16. Check it out!

Road to Vegas Regional

June 10, 2025 at 2:04 PM

The regional in Vegas is coming up soon and I have no idea what to play.

I don’t think Marco is as good into this meta. He has a good matchup into GP and UP Luffy. But I don’t think he has a good matchup into Buggy, and the rest of the playing field is a wild card at the moment.

I haven’t had much time to practice either, but I have played a total of 7 games. 5 of those with UP Luffy and 2 with BP Luffy. All of them were against my friends Buggy.

Currently at 2 wins and 3 losses with UP Luffy. 1 win and 1 loss with BP Luffy.

I have to say, Buggy is nasty. It’s such a strong deck and fairly consistent with all the hand filtering it does. So I might consider playing him as well, but with such little time and no reps in maybe not.

Between UP and BP Luffy, UP feels way more consistent. They’re both fun, but I might just go with consistency to play it safe.

Till the next one,
GGs

OP11 Prerelease #2

June 5, 2025 at 5:51 PM

My second prerelease went pretty well! I got 8th place which gave me an extra 4 packs. My participation and prize packs sucked, but my box on the other hand was pretty nice!

Between the 2 prereleases, i’m happy with my pulls. I might be playing blurple Luffy, and unfortunately didn’t get a playset of Nami’s or Katakuri’s from any of my packs. So I ended up buying the Nami’s, and got lucky with 3 Katakuri’s from packs.

Now to decide what to play for the Vegas regional… I’m still not fully locked in. I want to play blurple Luffy cause it seems like it’ll still be the top deck. But I’m also considering Marco or Buggy as well.
